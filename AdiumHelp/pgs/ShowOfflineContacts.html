Show Offline Contacts

By default, Adium hides offline contacts. Offline contacts include those that are currently offline as well as those who have set themselves as Invisible.

Showing Offline Contacts

  • If you want to see a complete list of your contacts including the contacts that are not online at the moment, select Show Offline Contacts from the View menu or hit the comand-shift-H hotkey. Repeat the process to hide offline contacts.
