MobileMe

Description

Homepage: http://www.apple.com/mobileme/
Type: Proprietary
Identity: user name (called "screen name" by AOL)
Interoperability: AIM, ICQ
Popularity: USA
Default server; port: login.oscar.aol.com; 5190

Formerly known as ".Mac", MobileMe is Apple's bundle of online services, including AIM-compatible instant-messaging.

History

Being the second modern client after ICQ, AIM was initially released in 1997.
