Adium Xtras

Adium Xtras add new sounds, images, and more to Adium, allowing you to fully customize your Adium experience. Visit www.adiumxtras.com to download Xtras for your Adium client. Types of Xtras include:

  • Dock Icons
  • Emoticon Sets
  • Contact List Styles
  • Message Viewer Styles
  • Sound Sets
  • Apple Scripts
  • Status Icons
  • Service Icons
  • Menu Bar Icons
  • Plugins
  • ...and more

Installing Adium Xtras

  1. Once you have downloaded an Xtra, simply double click the Xtra in Finder. Adium will launch and automatically install the Xtra.

Deleting Adium Xtras

  1. Choose Xtras Manager from the Adium menu.
  2. Select the desired Xtra, then click Delete.
