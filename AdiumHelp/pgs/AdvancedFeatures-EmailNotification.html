Email notification

On supported services, Adium can notify you when you receive new email on the address associated with your account. Adium will not notify of email for any other email account; Adium is not a mail client and does not intend to replace one.

Adium supports mail notification for:

  • AOL accounts
  • AIM accounts with AIM Mail

To enable mail checking

  • double click your account in the Accounts pane of the Preferences window,
  • click the Options tab
  • check Check for New Mail.
