What's new in Adium?

Adium 1.3 is here and brings with it many new features. Here are a few of the major improvements.

Contact List Search

Adium now offers a search field right in the contact list. Pressing âŒ˜+F (when using Regular Window mode) will bring up a filter bar and will narrow down the results as you type.


New Contact Inspector

The Get Info window has now become the Contact Inspector. I has undergone a complete redesign for 1.3. Some of the improvements include: better address book integration, improved display of multiple profiles for meta-contacts, and meta-contacts themselves are now managed right in your contact list.


Support for Facebook Chat

Adium now includes support for Facebook Chat. Talk to all of your Facebook friends without having to log in to the website!


MSNP 12 Support

MSNP12 support has been added in 1.3. This means that we now have support for one of our most requested MSN features; Personal Status Messages, and much more.


Memory usage and speed improvements

Thanks to the use of the Clang Static Analyzer, Adium now runs faster and uses less memory than it did in the 1.2 series.


And lots more!
