Setting up a Gadu-Gadu Account


To Create a Gadu-Gadu Account:

  1. Choose Preferences from the Adium menu.
  2. Click the Accounts button at the top.
  3. Click the + button.
  4. Choose Gadu-Gadu from the menu.
  5. Enter your user name and password in the Account panel.
  6. Enter your alias and choose an icon in the Personal panel.
  7. Enter the appropriate server and port in the Options panel (if you're unsure just leave the defaults).
  8. Choose your preferred encryption level in the Privacy panel.
  9. Setup your proxy configuration in the Proxy panel if you need to (if you don't know what proxy configuration details to enter you don't need to enter any).
  10. Click OK.
